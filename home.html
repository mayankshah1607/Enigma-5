About

ENIGMA is an online cryptic hunt where participants will be given about 25 questions to be cracked with limited amount of time. Enigma 2017 and 2018 were enormously successful with cut-throat competition of 1000+ participants around the globe but a clear winner in the end. However, the wolf on the hill is never as hungry as the wolf climbing the hill.
